A 200 Year Old Buckle and Button

April 30, 2020

Categories: History Walden, VT

I found both of these relics on the same day in April of 2016 along the edge of an old road. They were about 75 feet apart from each other.

Both items appear to be from the late 1700’s and were likely lost by the first generation of settlers in Walden, Vermont.

Front View

Rear View

   

The button is 30 [mm] in diameter and is made of a brass alloy called tombac.

Front View

Rear View

Both are shown on 5mm grid graph paper.
