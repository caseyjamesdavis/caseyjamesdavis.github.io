Getting Started in Electronics

April 17, 2020

Categories: Books

I aquired Getting Started in Electronics at the local Radio Shack in the mid 1990’s. It taught me the difference between series and parallel circuits and how different electrical components function. Mr. Mims’ handwritten text and diagrams made it less intimidating to learn about this complex subject.

The last chapter in the book has many projects and I successfully built the one called ‘Mini Radio Transmitter’ which simply sent an adjustable tone to a nearby radio.

I still continue to dabble in electronics regularly, mainly with sensors and instrumentation in a test engineering capacity.
